## FAQ

### Which plan should I choose?

- **Community**: Best for individual developers and small projects exploring the platform.
- **Professional**: Ideal for teams needing advanced AI capabilities and faster support.
- **Enterprise**: For organizations requiring security, compliance, and guaranteed uptime.
- **Managed Service**: When you want to focus on building, not infrastructure.

### Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?

Professional plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual enterprise contracts.

### Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer:
- 20% discount for annual billing
- Special pricing for startups and non-profits
- Volume discounts for large teams

### What's included in support?

- **Community**: GitHub issues, community forums
- **Professional**: Email support (48h response), Slack community
- **Enterprise**: Dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone & email, custom SLAs
- **Managed**: Full DevOps support, proactive monitoring
